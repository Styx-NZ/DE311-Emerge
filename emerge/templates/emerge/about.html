About Emerge Exhibition

The Emerge Exhibition showcases the outstanding projects developed by our students during their academic journey. Our aim is to provide a platform where innovation meets creativity, encouraging students to share their work with the community.

We believe in fostering a collaborative environment where ideas can flourish. Join us in celebrating the talents and hard work of our students!

Where Emerge Is Held

Emerge is held at Ara's Christchurch City campus located at 130 Madras Street, the event is held in the 2nd Level of the Kahukura Building.
Kahukura is directly on Moorhouse Ave and is home to Engineering and Architectural Studies.

When Emerge Is Held

Emerge is held 2 a year after each semester.
